About UrbanEdge

UrbanEdge is a dark-luxury streetwear label blending premium fabrics with minimal, striking design language. We produce limited drops and prioritize quality, fit, and subtle branding.

Our Process

We source heavyweight cottons and brushed fleece, use reinforced seams, and partner with local manufacturers for small-batch runs.

Values

  • Quality over quantity
  • Sustainable small-batch production
  • Timeless, bold aesthetics
